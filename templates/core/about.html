About Bollywood DancePro

Bollywood DancePro is a subscription-based platform designed to connect beginner and advanced Bollywood dancers within a dynamic, AI-powered community.

Our primary purpose is to teach basic Bollywood steps to beginners while providing a platform for advanced dancers to practice and showcase their choreography skills. The core innovation lies in our AI system, which takes full dance videos uploaded by advanced users and converts them into step-by-step learning modules presented by an AI avatar for easy consumption by beginners.

Features

  • AI-generated step-by-step tutorials from choreography videos
  • Interactive feedback on user-recorded performances
  • Continuously updated song library
  • Monthly virtual community performances
  • User-requested songs (we'll add them!)
  • Community forums and social engagement

Target Audience

Beginners: Indian diaspora, Bollywood fans, and individuals seeking dance workouts.
Advanced Dancers: Professional dancers, dance teachers, and enthusiasts.

Learn Bollywood dance with AI-powered guidance and a community of dancers! 🎬💃
